Lasagne

Here comes a description of how lasagne is made. It's an Italian dish so of course past meat and cheese is a part of it. And that makes it an almost guaranteed succes.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Minced beef
  • Carrots
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Permesan cheese

Steps

  1. First cook the minced beef.
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Add pasta to a glass dish
  4. Finish in the oven
